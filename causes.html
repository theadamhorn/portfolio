Check Out These Groups

In addition to the coding and web development work I do, there are causes that I care about. Some are very personal as they have helped me become the person I am today. Others are just doing some really neat things. I encourage you to donate to any or all of these organizations.

Big Brothers Big Sisters of America

As an only child of a single mother, this program helped me gain a positive male role model in my life. That bond has been a life-long relationship and has offered me more than I ever thought possible. His family truly is my family. BBBS does tremendous work. I highly encourage you to donate you money or, better yet, your time!

freeCodeCamp

Coding can be a gateway of opportunity for everyone. However, not everyone has the ability or financial means to attend college or enroll in a boot camp. Resources like this make the process of learning web development widely accessible in a well-structured process. freeCodeCamp offers a range of verified certifications in JavaScript Algoritms and Data Structures, APIs and Microservices, Information Security, Machine Learning with Python, and many others.
